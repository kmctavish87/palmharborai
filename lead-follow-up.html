Lead Follow-Up

Respond to new leads faster without manually rewriting the same message every time.

This automation can summarize the lead request, classify it, and prepare a first response so a small team moves faster.

← Back to Automations

How it works

  • Capture a form submission, email, or chat inquiry.
  • Summarize the lead’s need and urgency.
  • Route the lead to the right person or pipeline.
  • Generate a first-response draft with the right context.

Outcome

Faster response times, cleaner handoffs, and less admin work for sales or intake teams.

Home services

Summarize the customer’s issue, tag the service category, and prepare a response that asks for the missing details needed to quote the work.

Agencies

Classify project type, budget hints, and urgency so the right person can follow up with context instead of starting cold.

Professional services

Capture intake details, note likely fit, and create a clean handoff summary for consultations.

Recommended starting point

Automate the summary and draft response first. Keep final sending human-reviewed until the process is proven.

Helpful inputs

  • Lead source
  • Requested service
  • Urgency
  • Location or account context

Success metric

Measure response time, booked calls, and whether qualified leads reach the right owner faster.
