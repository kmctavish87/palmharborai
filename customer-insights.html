Customer Insights

Use AI to find recurring themes in reviews, calls, support tickets, and feedback.

This kind of report helps businesses hear what customers are saying without reading every message manually.

← Back to Reports

Useful outputs

  • Top complaints and praise points.
  • Feature requests or service gaps.
  • Sentiment trends over time.
  • Quotes and examples that illustrate the themes.

Outcome

Teams can prioritize improvements based on consistent customer signals instead of anecdotes.

Review mining

Group online review themes so you can see what customers consistently praise or complain about.

Support themes

Summarize ticket volume by issue type to identify documentation gaps, product friction, or training needs.

Call takeaways

Pull recurring objections or requests from call notes and use them to improve messaging or service design.

Recommended starting point

Start with one source, like reviews or support tickets, before combining multiple channels into one report.

Helpful structure

  • Top positive themes
  • Top friction themes
  • Sample quotes
  • Priority actions

Success metric

Use follow-through as the metric: are teams acting on repeat signals faster and more confidently?
