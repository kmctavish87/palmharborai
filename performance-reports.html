Performance Reports

Turn raw numbers into a short update people can read in a few minutes.

AI can summarize campaign results, website metrics, sales activity, or output trends into readable weekly and monthly reporting.

← Back to Reports

What to include

  • Top-line metrics and trend direction.
  • Wins, losses, and unusual changes.
  • Short interpretation of what changed and why.
  • Recommended next actions for the team.

Outcome

Leaders get cleaner reporting with less manual synthesis every week.

Marketing reporting

Summarize campaign results, ad spend, conversion changes, and next-step recommendations in one readable update.

Sales reporting

Turn pipeline activity into a short weekly summary with wins, stalls, and notable trend changes.

Operations reporting

Roll multiple spreadsheets or status updates into a cleaner report leaders can scan quickly.

Recommended starting point

Choose one recurring report and standardize the structure before expanding into more business areas.

Helpful structure

  • What happened
  • Why it matters
  • What changed
  • What to do next

Success metric

Measure time saved preparing reports and whether leaders can act faster from the summary.

Strong fit

  • Weekly and monthly KPI reporting
  • Campaign summaries
  • Sales trend updates
  • Operations scorecard rollups

Not ideal yet

  • When source metrics are inconsistent
  • When teams disagree on definitions
  • When data still needs heavy cleaning
  • When no one owns the action plan after the report
